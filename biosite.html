My Bio Site

Ami Lorenzo is a passionate and dedicated social worker who graduated from the University of Bellevue with a Bachelor's degree in Social Work. With 5 years of professional experience, Ami has honed her skills in specializing in Medicare. Her work has allowed her to assist clients in navigating the Medicare system, understand their benefits and coverage options, and advocate for their necessary care and services.

Ami takes a strengths-based approach when working with her clients and is committed to finding ways to address their concerns within the therapeutic process. She believes in cultural competence and regularly educates herself on cultural norms and beliefs, approaching each case with an open mind and non-judgmental attitude.

Ami is also skilled in prioritizing and managing her caseload, regularly reviewing her clients' progress, and maintaining organized records. She is experienced in handling ethical dilemmas and advocating for her clients, ensuring they receive the support they need.

Ami stays current with developments and advancements in the field of social work by attending conferences, workshops, and trainings, as well as being a regular reader of relevant professional journals and publications. She also values the role of families and other support systems in her clients' lives and works collaboratively with them to achieve the best outcomes for her clients.
